Jackie Elders

Web Design

Jackie Elders Jr. has an Associate Degree in Information Technology - Computer Programming and Software Develovment. He has completed many courses in Web Design and the Fundamentals of Web Design, His qualifications include: extensive training with CSS3, HTML5, JavaScript, C#, MySQL, Microsoft Office, Gimp, Photoshop, Inkscape, and Illustrator. He has the ability to set up a network and troubleshoot the network as well. He has ambition to stay in college until he has his Master's degree.


With the training he has had in CSS3, HTML5, and JavaScript, Mr. Elders is capable of producing responsive websites. He understands the differences between previous version and new versions of each language. His abilities include but are not limited to animations, navigation, utilizing color theory, calling classes and id's, building tables, linking text and images to links, and manipulating different text and font styles. Jackie can code a website from scratch using html, head, body, main, and footer tags. He has knowledge of different types of syntax including but not limited to canvas, embed, video, audio, div, span, table, and article. The list could go on forever. With CSS3 he can incorporate internal and external CSS3.


With an education in JavaScript and C# Jackie can write simple programs to suit a websites needs. Weather it's a calendar or a clock. With the help of Visual Studio, he can create programs to fix and handle errors and bugs. He can design widgets that cater to a specific business.


From the experience he has had from databases and MySQL Mr. Elders can connect a website to a database. This allows for data to be gathered. He also has a good understanding of how analytics work. Databases and analytics will help business's do more business.


Image manipulation is one of Jackie's strong points. Photoshop is his favorite, but he can hold his own with Gimp. Vector graphics are no problem as he is equally as good with Illustrator and Inkscape. With his knowledge in image manipulation, he can create animations, logos, banners, and much more.


With Jackie's ambition to learn he will go great distances. The mind is a terrible thing to waste and he know that. Even outside of school he continues to learn how to be a better coder. He knows this is a very difficult task, but is up to the challenge.
